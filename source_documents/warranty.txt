SmartHome Hub v2.0 — Warranty and Return Policy

Limited Hardware Warranty Coverage
All SmartHome Hub v2.0 hardware units are covered by a 3-year limited hardware warranty from the original date of purchase. This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use conditions. If your device fails due to a manufacturing defect within this period, we will repair or replace the unit at no charge.

Accessories Warranty
Accessories included in the box, such as the power adapter, Ethernet cable, and mounting bracket, are covered by a 90-day warranty from the original date of purchase. Replacement accessories can be ordered directly from our online store.

What the Warranty Does Not Cover
The warranty does not cover damage caused by accidents, unauthorized modifications, use outside the specified operating environment, or power surges. Physical damage such as cracked casings or broken ports voids the warranty. Damage caused by third-party accessories not certified for use with the SmartHome Hub v2.0 is also not covered.

How to File a Warranty Claim
To initiate a warranty claim, contact our support team at support@smarthomehub.com with your proof of purchase and a description of the defect. Our team will respond within 2 business days with instructions for returning the device. You will receive a prepaid shipping label for warranty replacements within the first year of ownership.

Return and Refund Policy
If you are not satisfied with your SmartHome Hub v2.0 purchase, you may return the device within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. The device must be in its original packaging and in unused condition. Returns received after 30 days or in used condition will not be accepted for a refund but may be eligible for warranty service.
