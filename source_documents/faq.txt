SmartHome Hub v2.0 — Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset the SmartHome Hub v2.0 to factory settings?
Hold the power button on the back of the device for 10 seconds until the LED ring turns solid red. Release the button and wait 30 seconds for the device to reboot. All paired devices, settings, and network configurations will be erased.

How do I add a new smart device to the hub?
Open the SmartHome app on your phone. Tap the "+" icon in the top-right corner and select "Add Device" from the menu. Make sure the device you want to add is in pairing mode (typically indicated by a blinking LED). Follow the on-screen prompts to complete pairing. Most devices pair within 60 seconds.

What should I do if the hub cannot connect to my Wi-Fi network?
First, confirm your router is broadcasting on a supported frequency (2.4GHz or 5GHz). Restart both your router and the SmartHome Hub by unplugging them, waiting 10 seconds, and plugging them back in. If the issue persists, check that your Wi-Fi password has not changed. As a last resort, perform a factory reset and set up the network connection again.

How many devices can the SmartHome Hub v2.0 control at once?
The SmartHome Hub v2.0 supports up to 128 simultaneously connected smart devices across all supported protocols including Zigbee, Z-Wave, and Wi-Fi. Performance may degrade if more than 100 devices are actively reporting sensor data at the same time.

Can I use the SmartHome Hub without an internet connection?
Yes. The SmartHome Hub v2.0 supports a local-only mode. All automation rules, schedules, and device controls function entirely offline. An internet connection is only required for remote access via the mobile app when you are away from home, and for firmware updates.
